{% trans "Western Region (Men)" %}

{% trans "Men's traditional clothing in the western region of Saudi Arabia is known for its elegance and simplicity. It typically consists of a white thobe worn with a bisht, often in calm colors such as brown or black with decorative edges. The shemagh or ghutra is worn on the head with an agal, giving a neat and refined appearance. The bisht is commonly worn during formal occasions and celebrations, symbolizing prestige and social status. Overall, the outfit reflects sophistication, simplicity, and attention to elegant details." %}
